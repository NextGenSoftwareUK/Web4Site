FOUNDING PRINCIPLES

UNIFIED BY DESIGN

WEB4 is built on four uncompromising principles. Every avatar, every API call and every byte of data flows through these constraints.

🪪
UNIVERSAL
One avatar identity, recognised instantly across every connected world. No account juggling, no fresh starts, no fragmented reputation.
👑
SOVEREIGN
You hold your own keys. Private keys stay local and auto-sync across your own devices — never custodied, never locked into a single provider.
🔗
INTEROPERABLE
40+ Web2 and Web3 providers behind one unified API. Switch chains, databases or storage networks without rewriting a single line of application code.
♾️
PERSISTENT
Karma, reputation and history are never lost. Auto-replicated across every available provider with automatic failover if any one of them goes down.
CAPABILITIES

THE FOUNDATION LAYER IN ACTION

WEB4 is the foundation every other OASIS layer is built on. These are the capabilities that emerge when identity, data and reputation are unified under one API.

🪪
AVATAR SSO
One OASIS avatar identity across every integrated world. Name, reputation, karma, level history, achievements and NFT cosmetics all follow you — one login, infinite worlds.
🦸
KARMA & REPUTATION ENGINE
Real-world and in-game positive actions earn karma that flows through every connected world, raising stats, unlocking superpowers and building a portable, provider-agnostic reputation.
💰
UNIVERSAL WALLET
Private keys stay local and auto-backup across your own devices. Public keys and wallets auto-replicate across providers — a decentralised, aggregated wallet for every connected chain.
🖼️
CROSS-CHAIN NFTs
Mint once, recognise everywhere. NFTs minted through WEB4 are queryable and tradeable across every connected chain and marketplace, without bridging or wrapping.
📍
GEONFTS & LOCATION DATA
Real-world GPS coordinates anchored directly to on-chain NFTs and GeoHotSpots. Mint, discover and trade digital assets tied to physical locations — the backbone of every location-based OASIS world.
🗺️
QUEST & SOCIAL SYSTEMS
Quests, clans, messaging and social graphs all live in the same identity layer as your avatar — so progress, friends and reputation travel with you instead of resetting per world.
🔐
PROVIDER-AGNOSTIC AUTH
Sign in with wallets, social logins, biometrics or classic email — WEB4 normalises every auth method into one identity, so apps never need to care which one a user chose.
📡
REAL-TIME DATA SYNC
Changes to avatar, karma or inventory propagate to every connected world and provider in real time — no polling, no stale state, no manual reconciliation.
📖
FULLY DOCUMENTED API
A complete REST API with live Swagger sandbox endpoints. Query any data, manage any asset, control any identity — through one unified interface instead of dozens of incompatible ones.
